<p>Along with my cousins, who are all supremely talented musicians, I spent most of my childhood covering British rock on the back porch as long as the neighbors could stand it.
<p>I lament the fact that I  don't play as much as I did when studio recordings and gigging filled my evenings and weekends for more than a decade transitioning from the previous stint teaching for more than a decade. 

<p>I find solace in Logic Pro X, where things that took me weeks to do with MIDI hardware and software in high school are reduced to a few effortless hours. FWIW, the Bosendorfer in Logic Pro X is worth the $199 price if that's all you got, but you get a train load of unbelievable value in every upgrade. I'd argue it's the highest value product that Apple makes.
