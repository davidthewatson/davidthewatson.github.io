I recall the progression being very similar to what <a href="https://nishankmagoo.medium.com/an-interesting-story-about-bill-gates-b5dc6b922c93">Bill Gates experienced at Lakeside</a>. Firt came teletype, then the Atari 800, then the Apple II, the TI-99 4/A, and finally an Atari ST - the first computer I could really do MIDI <i>and</i> programming with similar joy.  The Macintosh and Intel 8086 would wait for music school, and computer science textbooks, respectively
