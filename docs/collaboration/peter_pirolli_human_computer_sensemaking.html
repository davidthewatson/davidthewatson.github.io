Peter Pirolli on Human-Computer Sensemaking Models
The Challenges of Incorporating AI
  • Models of human cognition in sensemaking have informed the design and engineering of higher-performance systems
  • For the foreseeable future, AI components in complex sensemaking tasks will not be autonomous—they will work interdependently with human specialists
  • Mixed human-AI systems “teams” pose new challenges and require new designs
  • My bet is that those designs can be informed by new cognitive science research focused specifically on human-AI interaction in sensemaking tasks

-- Peter Pirolli
